## Shot Type Library

### Opening Hook

Purpose: grab attention immediately.

Common forms:

- eyes open
- head turns to camera
- silhouette reveal
- hard title flash

### World Reveal

Purpose: prove the MV has a cinematic world.

Common forms:

- wide stage shot
- character at center
- slow push-in
- strong backlight

### Performance Shot

Purpose: connect the character to the song.

Common forms:

- lip sync close-up
- half-body dance
- full-body dance
- hand gesture on beat

### Insert Shot

Purpose: create rhythm and hide weak transitions.

Common forms:

- eyes
- lips
- hands
- hair
- stage texture
- light beam

### Final Shot

Purpose: leave a strong visual memory.

Common forms:

- direct stare
- title overlay
- collapse to black
- slow backlight fade
