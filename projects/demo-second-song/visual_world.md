## Camera Personality

Choose 2-3:

- slow push-in
- handheld energy
- orbit
- whip pan
- snap zoom
- static fashion portrait
- fast montage
- low-angle power shot

## Avoid

- style drift
- random locations
- changing character identity
- overcomplicated props
- muddy lighting
